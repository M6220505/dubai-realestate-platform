Process

How the Pipeline Works

Every Sunday at 03:00 AM, the system automatically collects, validates, and processes fresh market data.

01
🕷️

Scrape Listings

Playwright scrapes PropertyFinder, Bayut, and Dubizzle across all 5 target areas with stealth anti-block logic.

02
🏛️

Load Gov Data

Official DLD transaction datasets are downloaded from data.dubai with a 4-tier fallback strategy.

03
✅

Validate & Clean

Every record is validated against strict rules — zero-price listings, missing areas, and invalid URLs are rejected.

04
🔀

Merge & Deduplicate

All sources are merged into a unified dataset. Duplicates are flagged, not deleted — preserving data integrity.

05
📊

Generate Summary

Weekly market summary per area: average price, median, price/sqft, listing count, and data quality score.
